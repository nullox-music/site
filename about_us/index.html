About us

We think the music industry
broke something.

Streaming exists for 30 years and artists still get fractions of a cent per play. Distributors take their cut. Labels take theirs. By the time money reaches the person who actually made the music — there's almost nothing left.

Nullox was built to fix that. Direct deals, transparent royalties, no middlemen. Every dollar from your subscription goes further toward the people who deserve it.

We're a small team from Kazakhstan, building something we wish existed. Still in development — but we're already working with artists.

What we believe

01
Artists should be paid fairly

Our PP10KL system pays $15–$85 per 10,000 streams depending on the artist's audience. That's up to 2× what Spotify pays — without a distributor taking 15–30% first.

02
Discovery should be honest

No paid placement. No algorithmic manipulation by labels. My Flow recommends music based on what you actually listen to — 15 mood vectors, built from real listening behavior.

03
Streaming should be affordable

$2.9/month. Less than any major platform. Because access to music shouldn't be a luxury.

04
Independent music deserves a home

No label required to upload. No distributor needed. You submit, we review, and if your music fits — you're in.
